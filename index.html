Camping in school

Camping

The camping is very enjoyable,fantastic and many new things are there

The long awaited camp held at the end of the academic session always a grand success. Camping in the school premises is just too much of fun for the students of classes III TO VIII.The various adventures activities like Rappelling, Burma Bridge, Wall climbing, Zorbing, tracking add to the excitement surly.The children start early in the morning with a short does not given by the teachers followed by a track through uphill and down. They enjoy 4 km. track thoroughly. Modern lifestyles, lack of safe play options, more concrete, longer school hours a variety of screens, these are just some of the reasons children do not get to play outdoors.The adventure camp is the best activity for them. They are on their own away from comfort zone. It gives them the feel of comraderies and much more. The teachers are always monitoring and leading a helping hand when a required. It gives chance performing in the culture show which they organise in a short span. Sitting around the bon-fire fills their hearts with warmth and love and helps in the kinship

Achievement of school

INTER SCHOOL HOUSE DEBATE
Many students participate in it

English Inter School Debate Competition (South Zone) was hosted by Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Kanpur on 28th July 2019. The venue for the same was Mother Teresa Mission Higher Secondary School, Kidwai Nagar, Kanpur. The topic for the debate was ‘Education 4.0 is a threat to originality of the students.’ The event witnessed a participation of 15 schools from the South Zone. The observers for the event were our Principal, Mrs. ShikhaBannerjee and Mrs. Y Cabral, Principal Methodist High School
